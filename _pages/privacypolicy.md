# Privacy Policy for Atlas

**Last Updated: March 24, 2026**

## Introduction

Atlas ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Atlas mobile application (the "App").

**Developer**: Dawid (Osanuo)
**Location**: Warsaw, Poland
**Contact**: osanuo.studio@gmail.com

---

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored in Your Private iCloud

Atlas uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across devices. All data is stored in **your own private iCloud container** — we cannot access it. This includes:

- **Trip Data**: Destinations, dates, budgets, notes, and trip status
- **Trip Items**: Restaurants, hotels, activities, transport — with categories, booking status, and priorities
- **Itinerary**: Day and time assignments for each item
- **Crew Members**: Names, flight info, and confirmation status
- **Expenses**: Amounts, categories, dates, and optional receipt photos
- **Wishlist Destinations**: City, country, photos, and notes
- **Visited Locations**: Country and city pins on your travel map
- **User Preferences**: Display name, currency preference, avatar photo

### Sign in with Apple

Atlas uses Sign in with Apple for authentication. We receive only:

- A unique anonymous user identifier (to link your data across devices)
- Optionally: your name (only on first sign-in if you choose to share it)

We do **not** receive your Apple ID email address unless you explicitly choose to share it.

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT track your location (coordinates are never stored or transmitted to us)
- We do NOT collect device identifiers for advertising
- We do NOT use analytics or crash reporting services that send data to third parties
- We do NOT share your data with advertisers
- We do NOT sell your data

---

## How We Use Your Information

All data is used solely to:

- Provide the core trip planning features of the App
- Sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud
- Enable real-time collaboration with crew members you invite (via CloudKit sharing)
- Display your travel stats and travel map

---

## iCloud & CloudKit

Atlas relies on Apple's CloudKit for data sync and collaboration:

- Your data lives in **your private iCloud container** (not on our servers)
- When you share a trip with crew, a **shared CloudKit zone** is created — only people with your invite link can access it
- Apple manages all encryption, access control, and data storage
- Your data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

---

## Photo Library

Atlas requests access to your photo library only when you:

- Set your profile avatar photo
- Attach a receipt photo to an expense
- Set a cover image for a wishlist destination

Photos are stored in your iCloud container and are never sent to our servers.

---

## Share Extension

The Atlas Share Extension (used to clip web pages from Safari) processes:

- The URL and page title of the page you share
- This data is passed locally between Safari and Atlas via a shared App Group container on your device
- No data is sent to external servers

---

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Since all data processing happens within Apple's infrastructure under your control:

- **Consent**: You choose to create trips and enter data
- **Contractual Necessity**: To provide the App's core functionality
- **Legitimate Interests**: To enable features like crew collaboration, budget tracking, and the travel map

---

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored in your private iCloud account using Apple's encrypted infrastructure
- We do not operate servers that store your personal data
- Receipt photos and profile images are stored in your iCloud container
- You can delete all your data at any time from within the App or by deleting the App

---

## Third-Party Services

Atlas does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

If you purchase Atlas Pro through the App Store:

- Payment is processed entirely by Apple
- We do not collect or store your payment information
- Your purchase is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

---

## Children's Privacy

Atlas is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

---

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: All your data is visible within the App
- **Deletion**: Delete individual trips, items, or all data via the App; deleting the App removes all local data
- **iCloud**: Manage or delete your iCloud data in your Apple ID settings
- **Collaboration**: Stop sharing a trip at any time to revoke crew access

### GDPR Rights (EEA Users)

- Right of Access, Rectification, Erasure, Data Portability
- Right to Object and to Restrict Processing
- Right to Withdraw Consent (disable optional features at any time)

To exercise your rights, contact us at osanuo.studio@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

**Supervisory Authority (Poland)**:
Urząd Ochrony Danych Osobowych (UODO) — https://uodo.gov.pl

---

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained in your iCloud account for as long as you use the App
- Deleting the App removes all local data; iCloud data can be cleared via Apple ID settings
- We do not retain any data on our servers (we have none)
